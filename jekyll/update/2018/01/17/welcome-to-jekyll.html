Old Portfolio Show & Tell

Jan 17, 2018

This is my old SPA portfolio built with good old AngularJS.

Every time someone pulled it up, a websocket connection would start transmitting and saving to MongoDB their conversation with my bot along with their IP. Its responses came from a loaded Google Spreadsheet via Tabletop.js.

There was also a webhook for Telegram that kept track of who was visiting from where. It allowed me to pick and communicate with a site visitor after turning off the bot and outputting my own responses from the Telegram app on my phone or my laptop.
